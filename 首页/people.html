Sichuan

With a long history and abundance of the famous mountains and rivers, Sichuan province,known as “Heaven on Earth”, is among the leading Chinese provinces in tourist industry. There are national major well-know scenic areas, such as South Sichuan Bamboo Sea, Qingcheng Shan-Dujiangyan, Huanglong Temple, Jiuzhaigou and Leshan Giant Buddha, in which the scenic areas have been included in the World Heritages. The Sichuan, with increasingly fascinating charm, gives guests and visitors from the whole world a warm welcome.
